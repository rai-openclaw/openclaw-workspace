# Economic Data Archive

## Purpose
Historical macroeconomic data for stock analysis and context. Updated quarterly or on major events.

## Data Categories

### 1. Federal Reserve Actions
- FOMC meeting dates and decisions
- Fed Funds Rate changes
- Powell press conference key quotes
- Forward guidance shifts

### 2. Inflation Data (Monthly)
- CPI (Consumer Price Index) - headline and core
- PPI (Producer Price Index) - headline and core
- PCE (Personal Consumption Expenditures) - Fed's preferred metric
- YoY and MoM changes
- Market reaction summary

### 3. Labor Market (Monthly)
- Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP)
- Unemployment Rate (U3)
- Average Hourly Earnings
- Labor Force Participation
- Initial Jobless Claims (weekly)

### 4. GDP & Growth (Quarterly)
- Real GDP growth (annualized QoQ)
- GDP components breakdown
- Recession indicators

### 5. Other Key Indicators
- ISM Manufacturing & Services PMIs
- Retail Sales
- Housing Starts / Building Permits
- Consumer Confidence (Conference Board)
- University of Michigan Consumer Sentiment

## Time Range
- **Start:** January 2024
- **End:** Current (updated ongoing)

## Format
Each entry includes:
- Date
- Expected vs Actual
- Market reaction (S&P 500 move, 10Y yield move)
- Key takeaways
