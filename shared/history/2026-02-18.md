## Lessons Learned (For Team)

### Technical
- **JavaScript:** Always validate brace balance before deploy
  - Check: `grep -o '{' file.js | wc -l` vs `grep -o '}' file.js | wc -l`
- **Docker:** Container rebuild is fast and safe for rollbacks
- **Testing:** Scout's regression suite caught no new issues (good sign)

### Process
- **Memory:** Without logging, knowledge dies with session

### Architecture

- **Protocol:** Mandatory session-close logging ensures consistency
- **Automation:** Daily check catches gaps before they accumulate
