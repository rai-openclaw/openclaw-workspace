### Drills to Try at Home

**Positioning/Spacing:**
- "Find the open cone" ‚Äî Set up cones as defenders, practice finding open space between them
- "Shadow play" ‚Äî Practice positioning while someone else has ball

**First Touch/Control:**
- Wall passes ‚Äî Receive ball from wall, control with 1 touch, pass back
- Soft receiving ‚Äî Have someone toss/pass ball, practice cushioning it (not bouncing off)

**Patience/Finishing:**
- 1v1 to goal ‚Äî Beat defender, then control, then shoot (not immediately)
- "Pause then shoot" ‚Äî Dribble, stop/pause, then shoot (builds habit)

**Defensive Body Positioning:**
- "Ball first, body second" ‚Äî Practice shielding: get between opponent and ball using body/hips, not hands
- 1v1 defending ‚Äî Focus on ball, use shoulder/hip to block, keep hands down
