### 4-Week Event Cycle (Regular Events)
The game follows a predictable 4-week rotation for standard events:
1. **Week 1:** Cesares Fury, Alliance Mobilization prep
2. **Week 2:** Eternity's Reach, Hall of Governors  
3. **Week 3:** All Out (Kill Event), Power Up
4. **Week 4:** Strongest Governor, Kingdom of Power (if eligible)

**Note:** Servers sync to this cycle at different rates based on creation date.
